Spinach And Strawberry Salad

Home

Description

This is a light dish full of flavours that you will for sure want to try and save to repeat!

A very healthy alternative for your diet for sure!

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches spinach, rinsed and torn into bite-size pieces
  • 4 cups sliced strawberries
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup white wine vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • 1 tablespoon poppy seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon paprika

Steps

  1. Toss spinach and strawberries together in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk oil, sugar, vinegar, sesame seeds, poppy seeds, and paprika together in a medium bowl; pour over spinach and strawberries, and toss to coat.
